Lethal Habit: A Metroidvania Sidescroller Created by Students, Now Available as Beta Download

Lethal Habit is a Metroidvania sidescroller that was created as a school project by a group of eight talented individuals from an informatics course. Combining their passion for gaming and rock music, they set out to create a game with a unique twist. The entire game engine is completely self-made and built up from scratch.

In a world where rock music is the ultimate power, you play as a rebellious guitarist on a quest to unravel the mysteries of the Lethal Habit, a legendary instrument said to possess otherworldly abilities. Explore a vast interconnected world filled with hidden secrets, deadly enemies, and epic boss battles. Will you master the power of rock and become a true legend?

Get ready to rock in this adrenaline-fueled metroidvania sidescroller! As a member of the band Lethal Habit, you must navigate through a dark and dangerous world filled with hordes of mutated creatures and evil bosses. With your trusty guitar as your only weapon, you must channel the power of rock to overcome every obstacle in your path.

Enter the world of Lethal Habit, a dark and twisted realm where rock music is the key to survival. As you journey through a sprawling, interconnected world, you'll encounter a variety of strange and deadly enemies, each with their own unique weaknesses. Use your musical skills to defeat them, unlock new abilities, and uncover the secrets of the Lethal Habit.

Inspired by the classic Metroidvania genre and the hit game Hollow Knight, Lethal Habit takes players on a thrilling adventure through a world of rock music and danger. The project is still in development, but a beta version is currently available for download. Over the course of several months, the team poured their hearts and souls into the project, putting in countless hours into design, programming, art, and music. Today, the team is proud to share their creation with the world, and they can't wait for players everywhere to experience the power of rock in Lethal Habit.
